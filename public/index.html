DATA VISUALIZATION DESIGN WITH PASSION

I am a computational ecologist and a data visualization specialist with more than 8 years of hypothesis-driven research experience and strong skills in data transformation, statistical analysis and data visualization. For all the steps involved I use mainly R, the tidyverse and ggplot2 to ensure high flexibility and a reproducible workflow.

As scientist, designer and consultant, my projects include static, animated and interactive visualizations, mapping techniques and infographic design to gain insights from complex data and communicate those findings to the world.
